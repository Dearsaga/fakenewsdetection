Help & Support

How to Use the Fake News Detection Tool

  1. Navigate to the home page of the Fake News Detection web application.
  2. Enter the text of the news article you want to analyze into the provided text box.
  3. Click the "Analyze" button to submit the text for analysis.
  4. Wait for a moment while the system processes the input.
  5. View the result displayed on the screen, indicating whether the news is "Fake News" or "Real News".

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Ensure Proper Text Input: Make sure you are entering the full text of the news article, not just a headline or a snippet.
  • Check Internet Connection: Verify that you have a stable internet connection to use the web application without interruptions.
  • Clear Browser Cache: If the application is not working as expected, try clearing your browser's cache and refreshing the page. You can also click the "Home" link to refresh the application.
  • Browser Compatibility: Ensure you are using an updated and compatible browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
